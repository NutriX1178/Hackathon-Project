The consumption of junk food is on the rise in contemporary society.
Many individuals are driven by a desire for flavor, often neglecting to examine the ingredient
labels or consider the potential long-term effects on their health and life expectancy.

Our project aims to simplify this process by creating an intuitive website that reads food 
labels and ingredients, providing users with clear insights into the nutritional value of the products 
they consume. With our platform, users can easily upload images of food labels, and in return, 
they'll receive detailed information about healthy and unhealthy nutrients.
        
The website will feature graphical representations of nutritional data, a calorie meter to track intake,
and organized information to help users make informed dietary choices as in our society there is less 
awareness of food label and Marketing companies also make it more complicated by using unnecessary 
chemical names like sugar can be written in 73 different forms.
        
By empowering individuals with knowledge about their food, we aim to promote healthier eating habits 
and enhance overall well-being.
        
This project combines technology and nutrition to create a valuable tool for everyone looking to lead a
healthier lifestyle.
