Frequently Asked Questions

QUES-1. What are superfoods?

>> Superfoods are nutrient-rich foods considered to be especially beneficial for health and well-being.

QUES-2. Is quinoa a complete protein?

>> Yes, quinoa is a complete protein, meaning it contains all nine essential amino acids.

QUES-3. Are avocados healthy?

>> Yes, avocados are high in healthy fats, fiber, and various important nutrients.

QUES-4. What are the benefits of eating nuts?

>> Nuts are a great source of healthy fats, protein, and various vitamins and minerals.

QUES-5. Can I eat eggs on a healthy diet?

>> Yes, eggs are a great source of protein and contain many essential nutrients.

QUES-6. What is the healthiest cooking oil?

>> Olive oil is often considered the healthiest cooking oil due to its high monounsaturated fat content.

QUES-7. Are sweet potatoes healthier than regular potatoes?

>> Sweet potatoes are higher in fiber and vitamins A and C, making them a healthier choice.

QUES-8. What are the benefits of eating berries?

>> Berries are high in antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, which can help improve heart health.

QUES-9. Is brown rice healthier than white rice?

>> Brown rice is less processed and retains more nutrients, making it a healthier option.

QUES-10. What is the primary ingredient in the Middle Eastern dish falafel?

>> Chickpeas
